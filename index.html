MasayaCo has spent over a decade perfecting the art of sustainable teak furniture — FSC-certified wood, handwoven patterns, heirloom quality. Every piece is made by skilled artisans and designed to be repaired, not replaced.

Shoreline Design is the exclusive Canadian importer. As a partner, you get direct access to their full collection with trade pricing, dedicated support, and white-glove delivery included.
